# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, respectful disagreement, constructive feedback, accountability, and focus on the health of the project and community.

Unacceptable behavior includes sexualized language or attention, trolling, insults, personal or political attacks, harassment, publishing private information without permission, and other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may clarify and enforce these standards and take fair corrective action. Report abusive or unacceptable behavior privately through a [GitHub security advisory](https://github.com/Shik3i/KoalaParty/security/advisories/new). Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces and when someone officially represents the project in public spaces.
